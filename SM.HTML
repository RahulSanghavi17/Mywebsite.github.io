About

SMART MUSEUM will establish a platform for enhanced experience resulting from interaction between visitors and cultural heritage objects. The platform incorporates secure adaptive profiling, on-site local distributed knowledge and global digital cultural information access. Developments will also include semantic Application, cultural heritage related knowledge search and the software for delivering the cultural heritage knowledge exchange.
