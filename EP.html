About

We simply can not live without our smartphones. We use them to email, shopping, and get directions, also another thing. So, why not install a budgeting app? Budget apps are especially to focus on developing the habit of tracking your expenditures. In many cases, it is useful for keeping track of our expenses because we always have our telephones with us.

Working

  • There will 3-second splash screen.
  • Select from Income \ Expense.
  • Enter amount looking into Date and Time.
  • It will by itself be categorized into different income or expense types.
  • It will also create a chart that can help you weekly or monthly.
